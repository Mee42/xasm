calling convention:

Callee is free to use any register.
Callee must reset the stack to the state it was before the `call`.
Callee must preserve ebp


E*X: 12 34 56 78
*X :       56 78
*H :       56
*L :          78


error outputs:
0: successful exit
1: unspecified error
2: only 512 characters are allowed in the input buffer
